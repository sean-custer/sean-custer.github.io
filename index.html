About me

I love discovering about Cyber Security and all the possible ways to exploit and protect against vulnerabilities. Currently a software developer, I have worked with numerous web, cloud computing services, and network security tools used to protect, infect, and inspect systems. I graduated with my MPS in Cybersecurity from UMBC, as well as a BS in Computer Science and a Certification in Cybersecurity Operations. I enjoy challenging myself through various means of problem solving, ranging from Cybersecurity and Cloud Infrastructure to writing a verse on the Piano. My passions drive me, and I love to find ways they can intertwine, ranging from Musical Generative AI, Python libraries that generate visual responses to sound (lucid-sonic-dreams), music analysis software (Cyanite.ai), sound responsive LEDs, and much more.
